About Us

Hi! I’m Bryanna, the heart behind The Cozy Cookie! I was born and raised in Canada, and I’m a proud stay-at-home mom to two beautiful little girls who inspire me every single day.

Baking has always been my happy place...a way to share love, comfort, and joy with the people around me. What started as a way to treat friends and family quickly turned into a passion project.

At The Cozy Cookie, it’s all about creating moments of sweetness. Whether it’s a warm cookie shared over a hot drink or a surprise treat on someone’s doorstep, my goal is simple: to bring joy to your day through fresh, handmade cookies that taste like home.

Thank you for being here. I hope you feel the love in every bite!


With warmth,

Bryanna

Founder & Baker, The Cozy Cookie
